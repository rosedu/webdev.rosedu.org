Sponsor: Eloquentix

We are a team of passionate technologists that join nimble teams to build and grow successful products. We believe in enjoying what we do, in developing for pleasure. If we deliver simple, clean and working software we have done a good days work.

Our technological interests vary. Some are Erlang passionatos. Some love clean backbone code. Some are adamant about well tested code. Our goal is have projects and an environment where each of us can express their talents.
