Sponsor: Eloquentix

We are a team of passionate technologists that join nimble teams to build and grow successful products. We believe in enjoying what we do, in developing for pleasure. If we deliver simple, clean and working software we have done a good days work.

Our technological interests vary. Some are Erlang passionatos. Some love clean backbone code. Some are adamant about well tested code. Our goal is have projects and an environment where each of us can express their talents.

Sponsor: Eau de Web

We are a Romanian software company specialised in Web development. Most of our work is open source and that’s the way we like it.

Sponsor: Adobe România

Suntem un R&D center, o filială a companiei Adobe, care deține multe tehnologii și unelte de dezvoltare web - Flash, Flex, AIR, Dreamweaver. Noi lucrăm la aceste tehnologii web, le dezvoltăm, le extindem și le ducem mai departe. Adobe România înseamnă aproape 200 de oameni talentați care colaborează strâns cu echipele Adobe din întreaga lume.
